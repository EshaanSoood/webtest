Dear Listener,

Nothing warms my heart more than knowing that even in the year 2025 you decided to get this CD. This album, Dream River, is one that has been in the making for 6 years. All the river's twists and turns have resulted in this music. Each song on this album is a short story from diﬀerent phases of my life. From the very first jazz song I wrote, Mountain Muse, to the latest Sailing Through Dream River, I hope as you listen to this album you are taken on a ride. A Journey through all the beautiful and not so beautiful moments in life - yours, mine, all of ours, captured and turned into a sonic concoction for you to send your memories sailing like paper boats. The reason this album took so long to make can be distilled to one word - belief. As the currents of the river took me diﬀerent places, my self-belief felt like waves, rising high before they crashed on a shore. It took all of this time to realise that these waves are the very energy that gives the water movement. I hope this discovery, the joy and happiness in riding the ups and downs of life brings some light and joy to your own river.

Much Love,
Eshaan
