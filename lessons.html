Get in touch

My name is Eshaan, and I'm here to help you carve a path towards your musical goals. And, I will be with you every step of the way. I started playing guitar to write songs and collaborate with my friends. Through that journey I have studied music formally for 10 years and played styles ranging from Pop, RnB, Heavy Metal, Funk and everything in between. My focus in my own playing is jazz and improvisation along with honing my compositional voice and an unquenchable curiosity for music theory.

why study with me?

global experience

You get to study with someone who has led multiple bands in multiple countries as well as been a sideman. Through the years I have carefully catalogued the necessary skills and how I learnt them.

rooted in music pedagogy.

You get a mentor who has been exposed to music pedagogy for over 10 years across esteemed schools like the Eastman School of Music, Global Music Institute as well as my time studying privately with teachers across the globe.

a place for everyone

Over the years, my students have ranged from 5 years old to 60 years, they have been from multiple ethnicities and have had their own journeys and struggles including physical and mental disabilities. Being a blind musician myself, I have a empathetic understanding of the value of meeting people where they are.

style agnostic teaching

I believe that a strong grasp of the fundamentals of music and guitar playing enable my students to be creative and free from style barriers.

what you get?

an individualised lesson plan

A step by step process to reach your goals

a holistic approach

We can work on things solely guitar focused or branch out to subjects like composition, music theory, recording, or aspects of the music business depending on your needs

accountability

Monthly Check ins to ensure your goals are being met

like minded community

Every quarter we have a virtual meet up with all my students. I also invite a fellow industry professional  to share their experiences and answer your burning questions. And most of all a teacher that is rooting for you every step of the way.

let’s talk

Give me a call and lets talk about what you want to be doing with music, if I can help, we can work together. I don’t make any false promises and only take on students I know I will be able to take to the next level
